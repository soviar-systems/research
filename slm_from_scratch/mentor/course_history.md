# Course History Blueprint
**Role:** `course_history_role`
**Objective:** Maintain a high-level chronicle of the learning journey for rapid session resumption.

## [MENTOR OPERATIONAL INSTRUCTIONS]
The Course History is a distilled audit trail. It is used by the mentor during the "Continuity Bridge" phase of subsequent sessions to re-establish context without re-reading the entire progress ledger.

## [HISTORY SCHEMA]
`[SESSION-ID]` | **Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD] | **Mastered ALO:** [The specific ID and title] | **Key Insight:** [The most important technical takeaway] | **Mental Model Shift:** [How the student's perception of the system changed]

### Recording Guidelines:
- **Distilled:** Only record the "High-Water Mark" of the session.
- **Structural:** Focus on the movement from "Unknown" $\rightarrow$ "Intuition" $\rightarrow$ "Implementation."

## [EXAMPLE ENTRY]
`[SESS-01]` | **Date:** 2026-05-04 | **Mastered ALO:** ALO-1.1 (Linear Neuron) | **Key Insight:** The bias is not just an offset, but a way to shift the activation function's threshold. | **Mental Model Shift:** Moved from seeing the neuron as a "black box" to seeing it as a geometric transformation in N-dimensional space.
